About Spacebar Clicker

A free browser clicker game — no download, no account, no cost. Press the spacebar, earn points, buy upgrades, and watch the number go up.

What is Spacebar Clicker?

Spacebar Clicker is an idle clicker game that runs entirely in your browser. The core loop is simple: press the spacebar (or click/tap the game area) to earn points, then spend those points on upgrades that generate passive income automatically.

As upgrades stack, your score grows faster and faster — even while you're not actively pressing anything. It's the kind of game you open for two minutes and close thirty minutes later.

What is a Clicker Game?

Clicker games (also called idle games or incremental games) are a genre where the main interaction is a repeated action — a click, a tap, or a keypress — that earns a resource. Over time, that resource funds upgrades which automate and accelerate the earning process.

The appeal is the feedback loop: each upgrade makes the next one arrive faster, and there's always something just around the corner. Cookie Clicker, released in 2013, popularized the genre and is still one of the most played browser games today.

The Origin of This Game

The Spacebar Clicker game hosted here was built for the js13kGames competition — an annual game jam where the entire game must fit inside a 13 kilobyte ZIP file. That's smaller than most profile pictures. Despite the constraint, the game includes a complete upgrade system and polished visuals.

About This Site

This site exists to give the game a permanent, fast-loading home with no ads and no distractions. The goal is a clean page that loads quickly and gets out of the way so you can play.

That's the backstory. Now go play.
